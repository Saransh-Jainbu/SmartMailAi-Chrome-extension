Features

Everything your inbox deserves.

Built on real AI, not heuristics. SmartMail reads what you're already looking at and makes it intelligible.

Smart Priority Scoring
Every email receives a 0–100 priority score using keyword heuristics and AI classification. Urgent issues always surface first — no configuration needed.
AI Summaries
One-sentence AI summaries for every email, generated on-demand and cached. Understand what each email is about in under a second, without opening it.
Polish & Draft Replies
Compose your reply, hit Polish, and watch it transform into a perfectly worded message. Or use Draft Reply to generate a first draft from your intent in one tap.
Executive Briefing
A daily AI briefing every time you open the panel — inbox health, urgent count, and the single most important action you should take right now.
Gmail & Outlook Support
Full support for both email clients via native content scripts. Reads your current inbox tab — no OAuth, no email forwarding, no mailbox access required.
Smart Filters & Search
Filter by category, unread status, or attachments. Full-text search across senders, subjects, and snippets — instantly, without leaving the side panel.
How it works

Up in 60 seconds.

Three steps, no account required. Your keys stay in your browser and your email never leaves your machine.

01 — Install
Add to Chrome
Install the extension from the Chrome Web Store in one click. No account, no email login, no permissions beyond reading the tab you're on.
02 — Connect AI
Add your API key
Open Settings and paste your OpenAI or Anthropic API key. It's stored locally in your browser — we never see it. Hit "Test connection" to confirm.
03 — Go
Open Gmail or Outlook
Click the SmartMail AI icon in your toolbar. The side panel opens, reads your inbox, and starts classifying, summarizing, and briefing you — immediately.

Zero-trust key storage
Your API key is stored in chrome.storage.local — encrypted, local, never transmitted to us. All AI calls go directly from your browser to the provider's API. SmartMail AI has no backend.
Privacy

Your email stays yours.

SmartMail AI is designed to be trustworthy by construction — not by policy.

No mailbox login
We never ask for Gmail or Outlook credentials. The extension reads the tab you're already viewing — nothing more.
No server-side storage
SmartMail AI has no backend. Your email data, summaries, and drafts are cached locally in your browser via IndexedDB.
AI calls only when triggered
Email content is sent to your chosen AI provider only when you explicitly request a summary, classification, or draft — never in the background.
Content-hash caching
Identical emails are cached by content hash so the same text is never sent to the AI twice — saving both your privacy and your API budget.
